Construct the common perpendicular to the two given h-lines using the following steps:

  • Choose two points, say A and A', on one line and drop perpendiculars, say a and a', from them to the other line.
  • Drop perpendicular from A to a' and from A' to a; let P be the point of their intersection.
  • Drop perpendicular from P to one of the given lines.
